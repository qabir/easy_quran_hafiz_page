Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Is Easy Quran Hafiz free?
Yes. The app is free to use and designed to help Muslims memorize the Holy Quran.

Q: Does the app work offline?
Yes. Once Surahs are downloaded, you can access them offline.

Q: Can I track my memorization progress?
Yes. The app tracks memorized Surahs and Ayahs to help you stay consistent.

Please email us with a description of the issue and your device model.
