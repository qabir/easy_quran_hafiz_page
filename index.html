Easy Quran Hafiz

Easy Quran Hafiz is a simple and powerful Islamic app designed to help Muslims memorize the Holy Quran in a structured and easy way.

Features

  • Memorize Quran with structured learning
  • Audio recitation with repeat mode
  • Track memorization progress
  • Multiple translations
  • Offline access

Privacy Policy

Easy Quran Hafiz respects your privacy. This app does not collect or store personal information. All memorization progress is stored locally on your device.
