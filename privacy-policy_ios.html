Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how the Easy Quran Hafiz mobile application handles information when you use the app.

Easy Quran Hafiz is committed to protecting your privacy. This application is designed to help users memorize and read the Holy Quran in a simple and organized way.

Information We Collect

Easy Quran Hafiz does not collect, store, or share any personal information from users.

The app does not require user registration, login, or personal details.

Local Data Storage

Your memorization progress and downloaded Surahs are stored locally on your device only. This information is not transmitted to any external servers.

Third-Party Links

The application may include links to external websites such as Patreon, PayPal, or other resources. If you choose to visit these websites, please review their respective privacy policies as they operate independently.

Children's Privacy

Easy Quran Hafiz does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. The application is safe for general audiences.

Data Security

Since Easy Quran Hafiz does not collect personal data, your privacy risk is minimal. All memorization progress is stored locally on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with the updated revision date.
